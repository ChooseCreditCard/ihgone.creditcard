IHG One Rewards: Hotel Points, Elite Status & Credit Cards

IHG One Rewards covers brands like InterContinental, Kimpton, Crowne Plaza, Holiday Inn and more. This guide explains how points are earned, how elite tiers work, what free-night awards look like, and how the right credit card accelerates your IHG points.

Best Cards for Earning IHG One Points

1. What the IHG One Rewards Program Is

IHG One Rewards is the hotel loyalty program for InterContinental Hotels Group, spanning luxury resorts to affordable chains worldwide. Members earn points on eligible hotel spend and partner activity and redeem them for free nights, upgrades and more.

  • Points – earned on stays, cards and partners.
  • Elite status – multiple tiers offering bonus points and on-property perks.
  • Free Night certificates – available via promotions and certain credit cards.
  • Brand portfolio – over a dozen hotel brands across different price levels.

2. How IHG Points Are Earned

You can earn IHG One points via:

  • Paid IHG stays – base points and elite multipliers.
  • IHG co-branded credit cards – earn points on everyday purchases and elevated rates at IHG hotels.
  • Partner programs – including airlines and car rental companies.
  • Shopping or dining partners in select regions.
  • Seasonal promotions – often doubling or boosting points during specific months.

IHG members who hold a co-branded credit card typically earn significantly faster, especially via welcome bonuses and category multipliers.

3. How Credit Cards Boost IHG Rewards Earnings

A strong IHG credit card strategy can multiply your annual point earnings. Depending on the card, benefits can include:

  • Higher point multipliers at IHG hotels.
  • Bonus categories on common spending areas.
  • Valuable welcome bonuses.
  • Automatic elite status or elite-qualifying night credits.
  • Free Night certificates valid at many properties worldwide.

For many travelers, the combination of an IHG card plus 2–3 IHG stays per year creates a solid flow of points.

4. IHG One Rewards Redemption Options

IHG points can be redeemed for:

  • Hotel award nights – dynamic pricing depending on season and property.
  • Points & Cash – mix points with cash for more flexible bookings.
  • Room upgrades depending on availability and rate rules.
  • Experiences or partner rewards in selected markets.
  • Free Night certificates from IHG credit cards.

Many members aim to use points during high-cash-price dates or at premium resorts to maximize value.

5. IHG One Elite Status Tiers

IHG One Rewards includes several elite levels, each with perks and point bonuses:

  • Club – basic member tier.
  • Silver Elite – entry elite status with some point bonuses.
  • Gold Elite – mid-tier with stronger multipliers.
  • Platinum Elite – higher bonuses and priority perks.
  • Diamond Elite – top published tier with the largest perks and recognition.

Some IHG credit cards grant instant Platinum or help accelerate progress toward Diamond via elite-qualifying nights.
